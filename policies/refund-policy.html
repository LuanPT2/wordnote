Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 2024
Effective Date: December 2024

This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds of Wordnote subscription fees and purchases. Please read this policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. General Refund Policy

Wordnote offers refunds under specific circumstances as outlined in this policy. All refund requests are subject to review and approval by our customer service team.

2. Subscription Refunds

2.1 Monthly Subscriptions

  • Full Refund: Within 7 days of initial purchase if no significant usage
  • Partial Refund: Prorated refund for unused portion if cancelled within 30 days
  • No Refund: After 30 days or if significant usage has occurred

2.2 Annual Subscriptions

  • Full Refund: Within 14 days of initial purchase if no significant usage
  • Partial Refund: Prorated refund for unused months if cancelled within 60 days
  • No Refund: After 60 days or if significant usage has occurred

2.3 Family Plans

  • Same refund terms as individual subscriptions
  • Refund amount based on number of active users
  • All family members must be considered in usage assessment

3. Refund Eligibility Criteria

3.1 Qualifying Circumstances

  • Technical Issues: Service unavailable for more than 48 hours
  • Billing Errors: Duplicate charges or incorrect amounts
  • Service Changes: Significant reduction in promised features
  • Unauthorized Purchase: Fraudulent or unauthorized transactions
  • Dissatisfaction: Genuine dissatisfaction with service quality

3.2 Non-Qualifying Circumstances

  • Change of mind after significant usage
  • Failure to use available features
  • Personal circumstances unrelated to our service
  • Violation of Terms of Service
  • Refund requests made after policy timeframes

4. Refund Process

4.1 How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact Support: Email refunds@wordnote.dev with your request
  2. Provide Information: Include your account details and reason for refund
  3. Wait for Review: Our team will review your request within 2-3 business days
  4. Receive Response: We'll notify you of the decision and next steps

4.2 Required Information

  • Account email address
  • Order number or transaction ID
  • Reason for refund request
  • Supporting documentation (if applicable)

4.3 Processing Time

  • Review Period: 2-3 business days
  • Processing Time: 5-10 business days after approval
  • Bank Processing: Additional 3-5 business days for bank transfers

6. Special Circumstances

6.1 Technical Issues

  • Service unavailable for extended periods
  • Critical features not working as advertised
  • Data loss due to platform issues
  • Security breaches affecting user data

6.2 Billing Disputes

  • Duplicate charges
  • Incorrect subscription tier charged
  • Unauthorized upgrades
  • Currency conversion errors

6.3 Service Changes

  • Removal of promised features
  • Significant price increases
  • Platform discontinuation
  • Major policy changes affecting service

7. Partial Refunds

7.1 Calculation Method

  • Based on unused portion of subscription period
  • Usage assessment considers login frequency and feature usage
  • Minimum usage threshold may apply

7.2 Usage Assessment

  • Login frequency and duration
  • Features accessed and used
  • Content consumed (lessons, exercises, etc.)
  • Overall engagement with the platform

8. Refund Exclusions

8.1 Non-Refundable Items

  • Gift cards and promotional credits
  • Third-party integrations and add-ons
  • Custom content or personalized services
  • Items consumed or downloaded

8.2 Time-Based Exclusions

  • Refunds requested after policy timeframes
  • Subscriptions renewed automatically
  • Accounts with policy violations
  • Fraudulent or suspicious activity

9. Dispute Resolution

9.1 Internal Review

  • All refund requests are reviewed by our customer service team
  • Complex cases may be escalated to management
  • We may request additional information or documentation

9.2 External Disputes

  • Credit card chargebacks are handled through payment processors
  • App store refunds are processed through respective platforms
  • Legal disputes may require arbitration or court proceedings

10. Refund Policy Changes

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be communicated through:

  • Email notification to active subscribers
  • In-app notification
  • Updated policy posted on our website
